About Rags n' Wags

Rags N’ Wags is a small cottage cattery. We specialize in producing exceptional traditional ragdoll kittens! We own a cozy country hobby farm in southeast Missouri where our cattery began. Posey, Chocolate, and Echo are now our established queens and gentle Blue reigns as King! Their kittens are part of our family and given loads of affection before adoption. We hope to personally deliver one of our kittens to the right owners soon! We are presently open to selling our kittens in several states: Missouri, Illinois, Kansas, Tennessee, Texas, and Iowa, are states we have sold to in the past. Most of our Kittens seem to find homes in Chicago for some reason. But we can arrange meeting ½ way between our locations if your location isn’t Chicago or St. Louis. Kittens may be RESERVED for $250 (non-refundable). Please use our contact form to express your interest and we will respond to you soon!

Registered Paperwork

All kittens are sold on contract and must be neutered or spayed between 5-6 months of age before registered paperwork is shared. In our contract we must see your scheduled appointment for the procedure before we can release a kitten. Alternately we will keep your kitten longer and alter him or her for you. However the cost of your kitten will be higher by $250 for a male and $350 for a female. You can obtain your kitten at 8-12 weeks of age if you contract to alter them yourself. We also provide your kitten’s first vaccination, deworming, and a health certificate

Blue

Negative: FLeV, FIV

Negative: HCM, PKD, PRA

Blue Bi-color

Blue is a very special Bi-color male that we brought home with us from Utah. He’s from the same cattery as Posey but totally different parents. His daddy is imported from Poland, a pure blue bicolor King while Blue’s mom was a seal bi-color from Turkey. Therefore Blue is European and absolutely carries the melt in your arms ragdoll trait to the max. You can do anything to Blue and he just smiles and purrs. European Cats are known for their extra gentle and “totally” ragdoll personalities. He was very expensive, and thus Blue’s kittens will be AMAZING!! Looking forward to sharing them with all the right owners who visit our website!
